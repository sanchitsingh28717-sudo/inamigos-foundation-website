WHO WE ARE

A Movement for Compassion & Change

InAmigos Foundation was founded on September 23, 2020, by Mr. Govind Shukla (Founder & CEO). It is a Section 8 registered non-profit organization, licensed by the Central Government, headquartered in Bilaspur, Chhattisgarh. We operate across India addressing critical societal issues through a dedicated network of professionals and volunteers.

🎯 Mission: To create lasting social impact through education, women empowerment, animal welfare, environmental conservation and social service.
🔭 Vision: An inclusive, compassionate, and empowered society where every individual has equal opportunity.
📚 Education 👩 Women Empowerment 🌿 Environment 🐾 Animal Welfare 🤝 Social Service

OUR INITIATIVES

Projects Making an Impact

Six powerful missions. One united purpose.

Ongoing

Project SEVA

Project Seva is our flagship social service initiative dedicated to providing food and clothing to underprivileged communities across India. Every meal we distribute is a step toward restoring human dignity and fighting hunger at the grassroots level.

Impact: 50,000+ meals and clothing items distributed

Ongoing

Project BachpanShala

Project BachpanShala bridges the educational gap for underprivileged children by providing quality learning experiences outside the traditional school system. We focus on basic digital literacy, life skills, and school curriculum support for children who lack access to proper education.

Impact: Thousands of children supported across multiple states

Ongoing

Project Jeev

Project Jeev reflects our belief that compassion must extend to every living being. Through a dedicated volunteer network, we rescue, feed, and protect stray and injured animals across our operational cities — because every life matters.

Impact: 50+ animals fed and cared for daily

Ongoing

Project Udaan

Project Udaan is our women empowerment initiative that works directly with self-help groups in rural communities. We focus on financial independence, vocational skill development, and menstrual hygiene awareness — empowering women to soar.

Impact: 900+ women empowered across rural India

Ongoing

Project Prakriti

Project Prakriti is our environmental conservation initiative dedicated to fighting climate change at the local level. Through tree plantation drives, eco-friendly farming advocacy, and sustainability campaigns, we work to restore nature's balance.

Impact: 20,000+ saplings planted across India

Ongoing

Project Vikas

Project Vikas is our skill development and employability initiative that runs structured internship programs across multiple domains. We have trained thousands of interns in real-world skills, helping them become job-ready and contribute to the economy.

Impact: 30,000+ interns trained over the last four years
